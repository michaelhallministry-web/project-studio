How Custom Projects Begin

Every custom project starts with a conversation about sound, direction, and purpose.

1. Share the Idea

Send a project request with where you are in the process and the direction you have in mind.

2. Talk Through the Sound

We’ll discuss genre, influences, mood, artist references, and what you want the finished idea to feel like.

3. Build the Production

The project is developed through sequencing, arranging, editing, virtual instruments, and creative production tools.
