How Projects Begin

Every project starts with a conversation about the sound, direction, and purpose of the music.

1. Share the Idea

Send a short project request with the type of music, where you are in the process, and the direction you have in mind.

2. Creative Conversation

We’ll talk through the sound, style, influences, genre, and goal of the project before production begins.

3. Build the Production

The project is developed through sequencing, arranging, editing, virtual instruments, and creative production tools.
